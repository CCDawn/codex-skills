# Literature Evidence Synthesis Examples

## Example 1

```text
Use literature-evidence-synthesis to turn these AMiner results into a literature matrix for multimodal reasoning papers.
```

Expected artifact types:
- literature matrix
- method clusters
- citation gaps

## Example 2

```text
I have papers, blog notes, and two benchmark repos. Use literature-evidence-synthesis to build experiment hypotheses.
```

Expected artifact types:
- claim-to-source map
- method comparison
- hypothesis shortlist

## Example 3

```text
Use literature-evidence-synthesis to prepare the related-work backbone for our competition paper.
```

Expected artifact types:
- related-work outline
- grouped citations
- evidence gaps to fill

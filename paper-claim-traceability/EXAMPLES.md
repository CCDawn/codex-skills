# Paper Claim Traceability Examples

## Example 1

```text
Use paper-claim-traceability to review our abstract and contribution list before submission.
```

Focus:
- strongest claims
- evidence links
- overclaiming risk

## Example 2

```text
Use paper-claim-traceability on the results and discussion sections of this benchmark paper draft.
```

Focus:
- result interpretation
- missing ablations
- unsupported causal claims

## Example 3

```text
Check whether every important statement in this competition report has a table, figure, or citation behind it.
```

Focus:
- traceability map
- missing evidence
- revision priorities
